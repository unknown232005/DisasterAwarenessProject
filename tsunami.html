Tsunami

Powerful ocean waves caused by underwater earthquakes and eruptions.

About Tsunami

A tsunami is a series of giant waves caused by underwater disturbances like earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, or landslides. Tsunamis can flood coastal areas, destroy property, and threaten lives.

How to Prepare

  • Know evacuation routes and safe zones near the coast.
  • Keep an emergency kit ready with food, water, and communication devices.
  • Pay attention to tsunami warnings and alerts.
  • Practice evacuation drills with your family.

How to Survive

Move to higher ground immediately if you feel strong shaking or see the water receding. Stay away from the shore until authorities confirm safety.

Tips & Tricks

  • Do not return until official instructions are given.
  • Stay connected via battery-powered radios for updates.
