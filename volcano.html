Volcanic Eruption

Understanding volcanoes and their impact on communities.

About Volcanic Eruptions

Volcanic eruptions happen when magma, ash, and gases escape from inside the Earth. They can destroy nearby land, contaminate air, and cause secondary disasters like lava flows, lahars, and ashfall.

How to Prepare

  • Know your local evacuation plan.
  • Have masks and goggles ready for ashfall.
  • Seal doors and windows to prevent ash from entering.
  • Prepare emergency supplies and documents.

How to Survive

Stay indoors during an eruption, avoid valleys and river channels near the volcano, and follow instructions from authorities.

Tips & Tricks

  • Wear protective clothing and goggles when outdoors.
  • Keep a radio handy for updates.
  • Stay informed about volcanic activity alerts.
