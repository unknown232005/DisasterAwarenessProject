About Cyclones

Cyclones are large storm systems with strong winds, heavy rain, and low pressure. They can cause flooding, damage homes, and disrupt life over wide areas.

How to Prepare

  • Secure windows, doors, and roofs.
  • Stock emergency supplies like water and food.
  • Stay informed with weather alerts.

How to Survive

Stay indoors during the cyclone. Avoid coastal areas and do not drive through floods. Follow instructions from local authorities.

Tips & Tricks

  • Keep devices charged for communication.
  • Have a battery-powered radio for updates.
  • Evacuate early if instructed.
