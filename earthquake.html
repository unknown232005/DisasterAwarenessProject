Earthquake

Understanding earthquakes and learning how to stay safe.

About Earthquakes

Earthquakes are sudden shaking of the ground caused by movements in the Earth's crust. They release seismic energy that can damage buildings, roads, and other infrastructure. Major earthquakes can lead to aftershocks, tsunamis, and landslides.

How to Prepare

  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances.
  • Create an emergency kit with food, water, and first-aid.
  • Identify safe spots in your home (under tables, door frames).
  • Practice earthquake drills regularly.

How to Survive

During an earthquake, drop to the ground, take cover under strong furniture, and hold on until shaking stops. Stay away from windows, mirrors, and heavy objects that could fall.

Tips & Tricks

  • Stay calm and think clearly.
  • Do not use elevators.
  • After shaking stops, check for injuries and damage.
