Wildfire

Uncontrolled fires spreading rapidly through forests and grasslands.

About Wildfires

Wildfires are uncontrolled fires in natural areas, often caused by heat, lightning, or human activity. They destroy homes, forests, and wildlife.

How to Prepare

  • Clear dry vegetation around homes.
  • Prepare evacuation plans.
  • Keep emergency kits ready with masks and water.

How to Survive

Evacuate early if instructed. Avoid smoke-filled areas and stay in safe zones.

Tips & Tricks

  • Wear protective clothing and masks.
  • Stay updated with alerts.
