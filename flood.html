Flood

Flooding is one of the most common natural disasters worldwide.

About Floods

Floods occur when rivers overflow, or heavy rain saturates the land. They can destroy property, crops, and infrastructure, and pose serious risks to human life.

How to Prepare

  • Identify flood-prone areas in your community.
  • Store emergency kits with food, water, and first aid.
  • Have a plan for evacuating pets and valuables.
  • Know local shelters and safe zones.

How to Survive

Move to higher ground immediately. Avoid driving or walking through flood waters, as currents can sweep you away.

Tips & Tricks

  • Turn off electricity if flooding occurs in your area.
  • Do not drink contaminated water.
  • Stay tuned to weather alerts via radio or apps.
