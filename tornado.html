Tornado

Violent rotating storms that can cause extreme destruction.

About Tornadoes

Tornadoes are fast-spinning columns of air extending from thunderstorms to the ground. They are among the most violent natural disasters, destroying everything in their path.

How to Prepare

  • Identify a storm-safe shelter in your home.
  • Secure loose objects outdoors.
  • Prepare emergency kits for family members.

How to Survive

Take shelter in a basement or interior room. Cover your head and stay away from windows.

Tips & Tricks

  • Stay tuned to weather alerts.
  • Protect your head and neck from debris.
